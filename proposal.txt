Website Development Proposal
Major General Mulugeta Buli Polytechnic College (M.G.M.B.P.T.C)
1. Introduction
Major General Mulugeta Buli Polytechnic College (MGMBPTC) is one of Ethiopia’s
leading institutions dedicated to producing competent graduates in science,
technology, and vocational education. To enhance communication, visibility, and
digital service delivery, the college recognizes the need for a modern, dynamic,
and secure website that reflects its mission, achievements, and academic
excellence.
This proposal outlines the plan for designing, developing, and deploying a
responsive and user-friendly website for MGMBPTC.
2. Objectives of the Website
The proposed website will aim to:
1. Serve as an official digital identity for the college.
2. Provide accessible information about academic programs, departments, and
admissions.
3. Enhance communication between the college, students, and stakeholders.
4. Showcase achievements, events, research, and innovation.
5. Offer online services such as student portal access, announcements, and
e-learning integration.
6. Strengthen the college’s image through a professional and visually appealing
design.
3. Target Users
- Prospective students – seeking admission, programs, and entry requirements.
- Current students – accessing announcements, results, and academic resources.
- Staff and college– sharing information, updates, and publications.
- Partners, alumni, and community – following college activities and projects.
- Government and stakeholders – monitoring institutional growth and
performance.
4. Key Features and Pages
a. Main Sections
- Home
- About the College (History, Mission, Vision, Core Values)
- Academic Programs (Departments, TVET Levels, Curricula)
- Admission and Registration
- News and Events
- Research and Innovation
- Staff Directory
- Student Affairs and Services
- Contact Us
b. Additional Features
- Announcement and Notice Board
- Photo and Video Gallery
- Download Center (forms, guides, publications)
- Feedback and Contact Form
- Integrated Email Access (for staff/students)
- Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
- Multilingual Support (English & Amharic)
- Future Integration: e-Learning Portal (Chamilo / Moodle)
5. Technical Specifications
Component Description
Hosting Local or cloud-based (college server or secure external hosting)

[www.buli.edu.et](http://www.buli.edu.et) if available)
Development Platform PHP, MySQL, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
Framework PHP
Server Environment XAMMP
Security HTTPS (SSL Certificate), regular backups, access control
Design Responsive (mobile, tablet, and desktop friendly)
Maintenance Admin control panel for easy content update
6. Design Theme
The website design will reflect:
- Modern educational aesthetic emphasizing technology and innovation.
- Color Scheme: Based on the college's official colors and logo.
- Theme: "Shine Through Education and Technology" — symbolizing excellence and
achievement.
- Tone: Professional, academic, and welcoming.

10. Expected Outcomes
- A fully functional, responsive, and secure institutional website.
- Improved communication and transparency.
- Enhanced visibility of MGMBPTC nationally and internationally.
- Increased engagement with students, staff, and stakeholders.
- Foundation for future digital systems (portal, e-learning, ERP integration).
11. Sustainability and Maintenance
The IT department will receive training to manage and update the website
independently. Periodic technical support will ensure updates, backups, and
security patches are regularly applied.
12. Conclusion
The proposed website will serve as a strategic digital platform that reflects
Major General Mulugeta Buli Polytechnic College’s vision for excellence in
technical and vocational education. By implementing this project, the college
will enhance its digital presence, streamline information sharing, and
strengthen its public image.
